Our Aim is to Make Teenagers realise that they are capable of anything and everything. We want to make Entrepreneurship a term that can be recognised by every teen. Talking about students, they spend most of their time doing school based activities, and what we mean by school based activities is mostly studying, doing home works, completing assignments, going to hundred different coachings and stuff like that. These activities exhausts a student's mind due to which they get tired and don't work on other things or acquire new skills. Schools suppresses a student's creativity, and don't teach things which can be actually helpful in solving problems. SocioLearn is an online Entrepreneurship school which helps teenagers can learn about problem solving in depth and learn how to use their mind creatively.
