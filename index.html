Who are we 📌


Sociolearn is an online Entrepreneurship school for teenagers. We help teenagers start their Entrepreneurial journey and scale their businesses. At sociolearn we believe that their are certain things which should be taught to teenagers but are not being taught at their regular schools such as Entrepreneurship. Problem solving is a skill that everyone should know hence Sociolearn
