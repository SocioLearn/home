About us



Teaching in schools should be in a way that students are able to apply their learning in the real world, but it isn't like that. The Online School is a school for students who want to explore education outside of school. We don't teach Newton's law of motion or atomic mass of Carbon but more fascinating things such as Entrepreneurship, Technology, Blockchain, Finance, Metaverse, crypto, Design, Creative thinking, and much more!
